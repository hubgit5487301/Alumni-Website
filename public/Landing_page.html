    About Us

    Welcome to the IET Khandari Alumni Portal—a dedicated community for former students of the Institute of Engineering & Technology (IET) at Khandari Campus, Agra, Uttar Pradesh. Established in 1998 as an integral part of Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar University, Agra (formerly Agra University), IET Khandari has long been at the forefront of technical education in the region.
     
    At IET Alumni Portal, we connect our alumni through exclusive events, job opportunities, study materials, and memorable galleries. Our platform is designed to keep you engaged and informed about the latest happenings in our community.
